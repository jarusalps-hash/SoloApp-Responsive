E-Delivery

GO PAPERLESS – Electronic Delivery of Your Policy Documents

Simplify your life and save paper. ALPS offers an efficient and environmentally friendly Paperless option for you to receive and view your Insurance Documents. With the ALPS Paperless option, your Insurance Documents are always available at your fingertips.

Consent to Electronic Delivery of Your Policy Documents (please review important information below and select one)
What you should know
  • You can change your Paperless Preferences at any time. You can opt-out of the Paperless option or update your contact information and email address at any time.
  • You can request a paper copy of your Insurance Documents. If you ever need a paper copy of your Insurance Documents, simply contact your ALPS account representative at (800) 367 2577 and ALPS will provide a paper copy free of charge.
  • You can download, save, or print your Insurance Documents at any time.
  • Hardware and software you need. To use the ALPS Paperless option, you will need an electronic device capable of accessing the Internet and a current version of a web browser such as Google Chrome, Firefox, Internet Explorer, or Microsoft Edge. You will also need a valid email address, a PDF reader software such as Adobe Acrobat Reader® (www.adobe.com), and a printer if you wish to print your documents.
How will ALPS electronically deliver my Insurance Documents?
  • ALPS will email your Insurance Documents to the email address you designate in your Paperless Preferences. ALPS will also post your Insurance Documents in your ALPS Account Portal.
What Insurance Documents may ALPS deliver electronically?
  • If you select the ALPS Paperless option, ALPS may deliver to you electronically any policy documents, billing statements, and notices regarding renewal or cancellation of coverage (“Insurance Documents”). Currently, ALPS provides electronic delivery for policy documents only, but intends to use electronic delivery in the future for billing statements and notices. By selecting the Paperless option, you consent to electronic delivery of all Insurance Documents.
Will my Paperless option apply to all types of insurance that ALPS provides?
  • Yes. If you select the ALPS Paperless option, your selection will apply to each type of insurance coverage that ALPS provides to you.
