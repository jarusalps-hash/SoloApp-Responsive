RELEASE OF CLAIMS INFORMATION: By executing this application, the Authorized Person hereby authorizes any prior insurer to release the applicant Firm’s claims information to ALPS.

If the applicant elects to defend a claim without involving ALPS in the defense of the claim, no coverage for that claim will be afforded the applicant Firm under the policy.

CLAIMS MADE AND REPORTED POLICY: The Authorized Person understands and agrees that the policy applied for is a “Claims Made and Reported” policy. Therefore, the applicant Firm must immediately report any claim to ALPS while the policy is in force.
